elm city eats

This page will host my reviews of New Haven restaurants. I'll share my thoughts about places I've eaten at with the hopes that it'll make your decision a little easier. I'm still experimenting on style so bear with me, as the content may update or change after initial posting.

reviewed

spring 2023

Hachiroku Hand Roll Bar

966 State St.
April 2023

cost: $$ - $$$
cuisine: japanese
group size: 2 (only bar seating)
day of the week: weekend
occasion: date night or an intimate 1:1 catch-up

Overall: exceptionally fresh, amazing hand rolls in a minimalistic setting

Tacos Los Gordos

167 Orange St.
April 2023

cost: $$
cuisine: mexican
group size: 2 - 3
day of the week: weekday (ideally taco tuesday)
occasion: a quick weekday dinner

Overall: amazing tacos freshly-made tortillas and delcious house chips; slightly pricier than your average taqueria but worth the premium

Miso

15 Orange St.
March 2023

cost: $$ - $$$
cuisine: japanese
group size: 2-6
day of the week: weekend
occasion: ideal for a medium-sized group dinner

Overall: craving the nobu menu, but it's too far away and more expensive

Barcelona

155 Temple St.
March 2023

cost: $$
cuisine: spanish
group size: 2-8
day of the week: weekday or weekend
occasion: celebrating something with friends

Overall: fun, relatively large tapas-style restaurant and wine bar with lively energy and great food

Hachiroku Shokudo & Sake Bar

261 Orange St.
March 2023

cost: $$ - $$$
cuisine: japanese
group size: 2-6
day of the week: weekend
occasion: you want japanese food beyond sushi and ramen

Overall: best japanese food in new haven (after the otaru omakase), with an extensive sake list

winter 2023

Heirloom

1157 Chapel St.
February 2023

cost: $$$
cuisine: new american
group size: 2-6
day of the week: weekend
occasion: a more traditional, nice dinner; good for parents visiting town

Overall: good but nothing too exciting; get the scallops if they're available

Bella's Cafe

896 Whalley Ave.
February 2023

cost: $$
cuisine: american breakfast and brunch
group size: 2-4
day of the week: weekend
occasion: saturday or sunday brunch

Overall: peak american breakfast food with impressively affordable prices; be prepared to wait over an hour

summer 2022

Camacho Garage

36 Fountain St.
June 2022

cost: $$
cuisine: mexican
group size: 3+
day of the week: happy hour if you can make it, weekday, or weekend
occasion: a casual night out with friends

Overall: great; try to take advantage of the indoor/outdoor hybrid seating on nice weather days
